About us

Paramount Autozone came into existence in the year 2001. A part of Paramount Group who is into a variety of businesses since 1981, the firm was awarded the dealership of TEL turbochargers in the year 2003.

Turbo Energy Limited manufactures turbochargers under license from Borg Warner Turbocharger Systems Gmbh in India under the brand of TEL. They are suppliers of turbo chargers to OEMs like Ashok Leyland, Tata Motors Limited, Mahindra & Mahindra, Eicher Automotive, Swaraj Mazda, Kirloskar Oil Engines Limited, General Motors, MWM, Escorts JCB, Ford, Fiat, Suzuki, BMW, Audi, Volvo, Perkins, Greaves, etc to name a few.

At Paramount Autozone, we believe that “Quality is of prime importance than Quantity” and in continuation of that principle we have achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification from TUV SUD.

Paramount Autozone has one of the best infrastructures in place with respect to the service of turbochargers in India for TEL and is working towards becoming the Numero Uno in the country with respect to the quality of service of the product. Our specialized tools and equipments and our team of factory trained and experienced technicians and our quality checks at each step of service ensure that the products that we service are returned back with the highest of quality and without damaging the precious natural resources of our nature, every time.

We have access towards individual genuine spares unavailable elsewhere outside the dealership network of TEL thereby bringing down the cost of repairs for your turbochargers.

We have serviced more than 10000 turbochargers till date and is growing at a fast pace. We are geared to meet and exceed the customer expectations in terms of quality service for their turbochargers and with an expanding footprint of service centers in the country and beyond.

Today, with its deep penetration into the automotive industry and the diverse and vast customer database which includes almost all major automobile dealers and service centers, Paramount Autozone looks into the future where it will be a one stop service point for all critical components with cutting edge technology!!
