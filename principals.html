Our Principals

Turbo Energy Limited : TEL was established in 1982. A joint venture between Brakes India Limited, Sundaram Finance Ltd & BorgWarner Turbo Systems Worldwide Headquarters GmbH (formerly known as K nle, K pp & Kausch), TEL is today a leading Turbocharger supplier to all Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEMs) operating in India.

www.turboenergy.co.in

Turbo Energy Limited (TEL) is a leading manufacturer of turbochargers in India, catering to the requirements of Internal Combustion engine industry. TEL is equipped with state of the art technology and skilled manpower to consistently deliver quality products. TEL has been able to achieve customer satisfaction by being able to provide products and services of high quality at globally competitive prices.

Borg Warner Turbo : BorgWarner Turbo Systems is a leading supplier of innovative turbocharging systems and a competent partner to the automotive industry worldwide. In the engine output range of 20-1000 kW per exhaust gas turbocharger, BorgWarner offers a broad range of products for passenger cars and commercial vehicles, as well as for industrial, locomotive and marine engines.

Since being formed when the well-known turbocharger producers 3K and Schwitzer merged, BorgWarner Turbo Systems has drawn upon a long tradition of advances in turbocharging technology. 3K and Schwitzer began developing and producing turbochargers as far back as 1952, and today BorgWarner Turbo Systems continues to set new technical standards in this field.

They also supply a full range of BorgWarner, 3K and Schwitzer replacement turbochargers and parts to aftermarket customers around the world.

www.turbodriven.com
