Trouble Shooting

We are providing you with a list failure symptoms and the probable causes so that your vehicle gets back onroad swiftly after a breakdown. We have seen that almost all the failures that occur on the turbocharger occurs as the resultant defect on the engine. Replacing / repairing a defective turbocharger may not solve your problem unless and until the root cause of the failure is identified and rectified on the engine. Once that is done, your repairs / replacement of the turbocharger will become more effective and you can avoid repeat failures which would cost you more time or money than necessary.
